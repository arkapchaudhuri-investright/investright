Arka Prava Chaudhuri

Founder & Chief Investment Officer

Arka brings over eight years of experience crafting transformative, data-driven products to his role as Founder and Chief Investment Officer at InvestRight.us. With a background spanning FinTech, enterprise software, and AI-powered products, he specialises in merging advanced technology with strategic planning to solve complex user problems and scale growth.

Prior to founding InvestRight.us, Arka led product and analytics initiatives at top-tier global organizations. As a Management Consultant at KPMG India, he spearheaded the zero-to-one development of an omnichannel B2B FinTech application — optimizing customer payments and loans, driving a 20% increase in Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) and significantly reducing time to market.

His expertise in scaling digital platforms was demonstrated as a Lead Product Manager at Sprinklr, where he managed a team of product managers and owned the P&L for an AI-powered SaaS portfolio, growing it to $38M in Annual Recurring Revenue. He has also provided strategic direction as a Senior Management Consultant focused on product pricing at McDonald's headquarters in Chicago.

Arka holds a Master of Engineering Management (Technology) from the Gies College of Business at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow, and a B.Tech from the National Institute of Technology Durgapur. His core competencies include inferential and predictive statistics, Python, SQL, and data visualization.

When he's not building financial platforms, Arka enjoys playing on chess.com, reading, sketching with charcoal, exploring new travel destinations with his wife, and passionately cheering for Manchester United.
