# Arka Prava Chaudhuri
**Founder & Chief Investment Officer, InvestRight.us**

Arka Prava Chaudhuri brings over eight years of experience in crafting transformative, data-driven solutions to his role as Founder and Chief Investment Officer at InvestRight.us. With a diverse background spanning FinTech, enterprise software, and AI-powered products, Arka specializes in merging advanced technology with strategic planning to resolve complex user problems and scale business growth.

Prior to founding InvestRight.us, Arka built a distinguished career leading product and analytics initiatives at top-tier global organizations. As a Management Consultant at KPMG India, he spearheaded the zero-to-one development of an omnichannel B2B FinTech application. This initiative optimized customer payments and loans, ultimately driving a 20% increase in Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) and significantly reducing the time to market.

His expertise in scaling digital platforms was further demonstrated during his tenure as a Lead Product Manager at Sprinklr. There, he managed a team of product managers and oversaw the P&L for an AI-powered SaaS product portfolio, successfully elevating its growth to a $38 million Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR). Additionally, Arka has provided critical strategic direction as a Senior Management Consultant focusing on product pricing at McDonald's headquarters in Chicago.

Arka's technical and operational acumen is supported by a robust academic foundation. He holds a Master of Engineering Management (Technology) from the Gies College of Business at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow, and a Bachelor of Technology from the National Institute of Technology Durgapur. His core competencies include inferential and predictive statistics, Python, SQL, and comprehensive data visualization.

When he is not building innovative financial platforms, Arka enjoys playing on chess.com, reading, sketching with charcoal, exploring new travel destinations with his wife, and passionately cheering for Manchester United.
